Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the AI summaries?
Our AI achieves 95% accuracy in preserving key information and context. We continuously train our models on diverse datasets to improve performance.
What file formats does Omega support?
Omega supports PDF, DOCX, TXT, RTF, and HTML files. We're constantly adding support for more formats based on user feedback.
Is my data secure?
Yes, all documents are encrypted in transit and at rest. We don't store your documents after processing, and our servers are SOC 2 certified.
Can I customize the summary length?
Absolutely! You can set custom word counts, choose bullet points vs. paragraphs, and adjust the level of detail in your summaries.
Do you offer API access?
Yes, our Pro and Team plans include full API access with comprehensive documentation and SDKs for popular programming languages.
